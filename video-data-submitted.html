Video Data Received

Depending on how many other submissions are in the queue, it can take up to 24 hours for your video data to be processed and a report sent by e-mail.

It is highly recommended that you reach out to fellow YouTubers who produce videos similar to yours, especially if they have a lot more subscribers and are actively uploading, and tell them about Awdisea. The more YouTubers with content similar to yours who use Awdisea, the more accurate the prediction will be. When YouTubers of similar channels use Awdisea, they collectively increase each other's channel's growth as a community. This is far better than each channel trying to go it alone.
