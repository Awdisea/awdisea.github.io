Verify your channel

Awdisea only lets you use its service for channels you own. You need to verify the channel that you own. The thumbnails and titles you provide Awdisea will only apply to your channel. If you've already verified your channel, you can just click on next.

To verify that you are the channel owner, enter your channel's handle. For example, you're channel handle would look something like "YouTube/@MrBeast". After entering your channel handle, click on the Create ID button. This will create a unique ID for your channel. Copy the text and ID and paste it into your channel's description. The description is found in YouTube Studio under the Customization tab. This will appear to anyone viewing your channel, so you might want to put it last in your description.
