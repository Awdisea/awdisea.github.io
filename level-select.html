Awdisea only lets you use its service for channels you own. You need to verify the channel that you own. Any YouTube data that you provide to Awdisea such as thumbnails and titles will only apply to your channel. If you've already verified your channel, just click the test level that you want to use to test out your video.

To verify that you are the channel owner, go to YouTube Studio on the channel that you want to verify. Under the Customization tab you will find a field called Channel URL. Copy the URL and paste it below. A URL will be created below it. Copy this URL. In YouTube Studio, create a link under Customization. Give the link the title "Awdisea" and then paste the newly created URL that points to Awdisea. This link must always be present whenever you use this service.
