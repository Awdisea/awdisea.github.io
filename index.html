Awdisea

AI for predicting YouTube video views in pre-production

Walt Disney

Awdisea is an AI powered tool that can predict how many views a video will most likely get within a week, even before the video is created. At the very minimum, it can predict the number of views solely on the videoâ€™s title and thumbnail, giving the least accurate prediction, or it can achieve a much higher accuracy when you provide additional data. Behind the scenes, Awdisea will also access access a lot of other data in order to increase the accuracy.

Walt Disney faced the problem of investing a lot of money in an animated film only to watch it bomb. He solved the problem by creating rough mockups of his films before expensive animation was done. Only when the mockup was well received was the animation green lit. Awdisea helps YouTubers take the same approach by testing their video ideas before beginning any actual production.

Publishing a video and hoping it will be successful is just rolling the dice. Use Awdisea to increase your chances of creating a successful video.

REQUIREMENTS

This is currently a free service but you must meet the requirements to use it. You must be the owner of a YouTube channel, have at least 500 subscribers and have posted at least 3 videos in the past 3 months (shorts are not allowed).

To verify that you are the channel owner, go to YouTube Studio on the channel that you want to verify. Under the Customization tab you will find a field called Channel URL. Copy the URL and paste it below. A URL will be created below it. Copy this URL. In YouTube Studio, create a link under Customization. Give the link the title "Awdisea" and then paste the newly created URL that points to Awdisea. This link must always be present whenever you use this service. If you've already verified your channel, you can skip this step.

VIDEO DATA

Each YouTuber can only make one submission a day. The more information you provide, the more accurate the prediction will be. It can take up to 24 hours for an assessment to be made. A report will be sent to the email address you provide below. You can define up to 3 thumbnails and 3 titles. After submitting your video data, it can take up to 24 hours for it to be processed. A report will be sent to the email address you provide below. Each thumbnail and title will be given a score. It is recommended that you use the thumbnail and title with the highest score. It is important that you use only one of the thumbnails and titles you provide during your submission and not change either of them for the first week after you publish your video, otherwise Awdisea will have no way of improving the data for future predictions.
